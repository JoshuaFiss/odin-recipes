Odin Lasagna

Description

This is a faster, and less expensive asagna

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ pound shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente;drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper;drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlice and simmer 5 minutes
  3. in a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, cheddar, and ricotta;stir well. In a 9x13 inch pane, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
